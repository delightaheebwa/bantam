# Interpreters & Virtual Machines

This repo is me learning to build a tree-walk interpreter and a bytecode virtual machine.

## Why?

I'm currently exploring the systems side of software engineering, and I strongly believe getting good at it means building foundational things... the software that's literally holding up basically everything you use today. These two are among them, and they're the first I'm tackling.

Beyond that, I genuinely want to. There's something in me that's drawn to exploring this kind of thing. I also believe implementing these will change how I think about solving problems... sharpen my engineering sense and judgment. That feels especially important right now, where I think a certain kind of deep thinking will matter a lot more given how much AI is everywhere.

Plus I just really love it.
